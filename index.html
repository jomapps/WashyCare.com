How WashyCare Works

Schedule

Schedule a pickup for any day of the week, including Sundays, mornings or evenings. And have your laundry delivered same day or even overnight. Or schedule maid service as early as the next day. WashyCare professionals are employed, background checked, COVID tested and fully prepared to service.

Pickup & Clean

Your WashyCare Concierge will stop by with everything you need to get started - personal free WashyCare bag for each service we offer: Dry Cleaning, Wash & Fold, and Hang Dry. WashyCare has a decade of experience in laundry service insuring that your clothes receive the highest-quality care. We’ll check your pockets and remove pens, receipts, and other items that don’t belong. Your clothes are in good hands.

Delivery

You will receive an email with the your orders total information. Once your WashyCare Concierge is on the way, you will receive a text message with estimated time of arrival and the gps tracker so you could follow your concierge movements.
